Toll Blending

What is Toll Blending?

Toll blending, also known as contract manufacturing or contract blending is a service whereby the production of (complex) chemical products is out sourced to a third party company.

DBM Chemicals Limited offers an independent toll blending/manufacturing service with complete confidentiality assured, protected by secrecy/non-disclosure agreements if required. Blending facilities include modern stainless steel mixing vessels with a capacity of 1,000 to 20,000 litres and all are equipped with heating, circulation, agitation, filtration, extraction and load cells.
