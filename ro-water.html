RO water is the result of the reverse osmosis process used to remove contaminants from water by pushing it through a pressured, semi-permeable membrane, RO water can be used as a pure water supply in a number of industry sectors.

DBM Chemicals Limited

Own and operate our very own state of the art reverse osmosis system with the capabilties of producing 2000 litres per hour.

The RO water is primarily used in the the toll blending operation carried out onsite but can also be purchased for use in the industrial sector. We currently offer options of 1000 litre IBC or bulk tanker ranging from 10,000 liters up to 20,000 litres.
