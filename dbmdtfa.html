DBM DTFA


DBM DTFA is a distilled tallow fatty acid derived from animal fat,more particularly of bovine origin. The acids are a mixture of saturated and unsaturated fatty acids.

DBM DTFA, like all fatty acids, has a wide variety of functions and is often used as a chemical intermidiate and processing aid. DBM DTFA is recommended as a lubricity additive and gelling agent in metal polishes and buffing compounds, and alsoas lubricity additive to hydraulic fluids.
