DBM MO


DBM MO is a non-staining, non-gumming and virtually non-carbon forming, high-lubricity fatty additive with high polar characteristics giving high wetting, spreading and penetrating ability. The high lubricity coupled with clean burn-off properties make it a superior treatment to conventional fatty oils used in drawing lubricants and other metal forming and rolling oils where non-staining on annealing is an important requirement.

DBM MO may be used for compunding base oils for machining operations where fluids of high oiliness but with out EP properties are desirable. Such fatted and super fatted oils of very low viscosity are suitable for high speed machining operations on aluminium, copper and their alloys, and honing and super fininshing operations. The higher viscosity compounded oils are suitable for the more arduous operations on tougher aluminium and cooper alloys and in general machining of mild steels.

The low viscosity and high spreading ability of DBM MO make it admirably suited to formulating penetrating and release oils and general-purpose compounded lubricants for threaded components, springs, locks, hinges, etc. DBM MO is also employed as a compounded aid to die lubrication in wire drawing emulsions and for improved tool life with soluble cutting oils used with both ferrous and non-ferrous metals.
